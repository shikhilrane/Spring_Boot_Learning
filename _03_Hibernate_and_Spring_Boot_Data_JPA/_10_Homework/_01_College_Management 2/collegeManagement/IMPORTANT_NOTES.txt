Mapping :
    1. Many to Many :
        i. Check where is FK Constraints, and make that entity owning side
        ii. If FK present or absent in both, then decide which one is more relevant to own the side, and make that entity owning side
    2. One to One :
        i. Check where is FK Constraints, and make that entity owning side
        ii. If FK present or absent in both, then decide which one is more relevant to own the side, and make that entity owning side
    3. One to Many OR Many to One :
        i. FK is always on the Many side â†’ Many side is the owning side
        ii. If designing from scratch, FK will go to the Many side, so Many side must be owning

Cascade :
    1. Many to Many :
        No need to Specify cascading
    2. One to One :
        At owning side
    3. One to Many :
        At One side
    4. Many to One :
        No need to Specify cascading

orphanRemoval :
    1. Many to Many :
        No need to Specify orphanRemoval
    2. One to One :
        At owning side
    3. One to Many :
        At One side
    4. Many to One :
        No need to Specify orphanRemoval
